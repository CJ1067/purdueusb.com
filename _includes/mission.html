Our Mission

Events

We put on events that matter. From small events like informal mixers with Corporate Partners to large scale hackathons like BoilerMake.

Resources

We're here to help the CS students. We have interview workshops for students. We also run a free help room for intro CS courses 4 nights a week.

Tools

We provide students with the tools and know-how needed to be successful in CS. We started and teach a class, CS 190, that is required for all freshmen.
