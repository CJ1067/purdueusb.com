Demo Day
Show off your project and win prizes

Demo Day is a chance to show off something that you've made. It can be a website, a mobile app, a robot, even a steering wheel drumset attachment. Come, show it off, and win some cool prizes. Projects do not have to be in a perfect "shippable" state - if you want to show it off, we're happy to have you.

If you don't have a project, stop by to take a look at what your classmates have been working on.

There will also be pizza!
