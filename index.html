What we're about
Events

We put on events that matter, from fun events like the Kanban Challenge to professional opportunities like the Corporate Partners Mixer.

Resources

We're here to help CS students. We host interview workshops for students, run a free help room for intro CS courses, and maintain a tutor list for those who need a little extra help.

Tools

We provide students with tools and know-how needed to be successful in CS. We started and teach a class, CS193, that covers essential skills not included in core courses.
