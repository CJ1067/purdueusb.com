What we're about
Advocacy

We care about your experience in Purdue CS/DS- like, really care. Visit our daily office hours or attend the undergraduate town hall to discuss ways to improve our community and student experience.

Tools

We provide students with tools and know-how needed to be successful in CS and DS. We started and teach a class, CS 193, that covers essential skills not included in core courses.

Resources

We're here to help CS and DS students. We host a panel during CS191, run a free help room for intro CS courses, and maintain a tutor list for those who need a little extra help.
