SHADOW DAY

FEBRUARY 10, 2017  |  11:30 AM to 6:30 PM  |  Monument Circle, Indianapolis

What is Shadow Day?

Shadow Day is an event for students who want to learn more about the ins and outs of working at a tech company. For those who haven't had an internship yet, this is the opportunity to have your questions answered and get exposure what it's like to work in the tech industry.

What will I be doing?

Students will be visiting the Techpoint office in downtown Indianapolis and one of three companies to shadow employees. During the shadow, students will have the chance to tour the office space, talk with employees, ask questions, and learn about the opportunites available for CS graduates.
